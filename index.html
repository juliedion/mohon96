Best of ’96

Remember what was happening in the world the year we graduated?

🎬
#1 Box Office Movie
Independence Day
Will Smith saved the world — $817M worldwide. “Welcome to Earth!”
🎵
#1 Song of the Year
Macarena
Los Del Rio sat at #1 for 14 weeks. You know you still know the dance.
📺
Top TV Show
Seinfeld
A show about nothing — consistently America’s #1 show. No soup for you!
🏈
Super Bowl XXX
Dallas Cowboys
Cowboys def. Steelers 27–17. Larry Brown named Super Bowl MVP.
🏀
NBA Champions
Chicago Bulls
72-win regular season + championship. Michael Jordan’s 4th title.
🏅
Summer Olympics
Atlanta, Georgia
The Centennial Games. USA topped the medal count with 101 total medals.
🎤
Grammy — Album of the Year
Jagged Little Pill
Alanis Morissette. Isn’t it ironic? Every Warrior had this cassette.
🎮
Gaming
Nintendo 64 Launched
Sept 29, 1996 in the US. Super Mario 64 blew everyone’s minds.
⛽
Gas Price
$1.23 / Gallon
Filling up a tank was an afterthought. Oh, those were the days.
💻
Tech Milestone
Hotmail Launched
July 4, 1996. Free email for everyone — the dawn of web-based email.
💵
Minimum Wage
$4.75 / Hour
The federal minimum when we were flipping burgers after school.
🎟
Movie Ticket
$4.42 Average
A weekend at the movies for two cost less than a coffee today.
